Programming

When I was learning about Java, I created many programs but the two that I loved the most were my U.S. Dollar to Japanese Yen conversion program and my tic-tac-toe program. The conversion program made me use the most math yet on a program. The tic-tac-toe program was hands down the most difficult one I've done yet but it was very rewarding after getting it to work.


Skills I've Learned

When I was first introduced to Java, I was very confused because it was the first time ever doing anything programming related. After writing my first "Hello World!" code, I felt very confident and found out that coding was going to be easier than what I first imagined it to be. I later learned about concepts such as arrays, loops, methods, cases, subclasses, and switches.
